How We Brew Coffee

  1. Heat water to 70 to 80 C (not boiling)
  2. Add 3 tablespoons freshly ground coffee to filter
  3. Slowly pour hot water in circular motions
  4. Let it brew for 5-7 minutes
  5. Add 1/4 glass cold milk to tumbler
  6. Pour hot coffee from height to create froth
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy the aroma
